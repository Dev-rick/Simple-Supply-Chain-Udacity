    The SupplyChain has the following steps:
  1. Item harvested by a farmer
  2. Item processed by a farmer
  3. Item packed by a farmer
  4. Item sold by a farmer (must set a price)
  5. Item bought by a distributor
  6. Item shipped by a distributor
  7. Item received by a retailer
  8. Item purchased by a customer
