Fair Trade Coffee

Prove the authenticity of coffee using the Ethereum blockchain.

The Supply Chain Process:

  1. Item is harvested by a farmer
  2. Item is processed by a farmer
  3. Item is packed by a farmer
  4. Item is sold by a farmer (must set a price)
  5. Item is bought by a distributor
  6. Item is shipped by a distributor
  7. Item is received by a retailer
  8. Item is purchased by a customer
